Our Story

Taste, touch, aroma — Corazón is an experience for all the senses.

Corazón serves modern, Hispanic-inspired coffee with a strong focus on pairing bold, vibrant flavors with the highest quality, sustainable beans. Meticulous attention to detail remains our hallmark, honoring rich traditions while elevating your daily ritual in a sleek, unforgettable environment.

CRAFTSMANSHIP

Craftsmanship is the balance of tradition and innovation. Corazón welcomes guests into a warm, modern sanctuary.

Warm copper accents, deep custom woodwork, and subtle grayish-blue elements shape a seductive, modern-organic aesthetic. Every detail is curated to elevate both the coffee and the social experience.

ON THE MENU

Our menu is a celebration of origin. From ethically sourced reserve espressos to our signature house-made Café de Olla and perfectly balanced Horchata Lattes.

Culinary delights continue with our freshly baked Pan Dulce and churros, crafted daily to pair flawlessly with your beverage of choice.

THE SOURCE

Farm-to-cup is not just a phrase; it is our foundation. We partner directly with small-batch farmers across Latin America to ensure our beans are grown organically and harvested with care.

Roasted in-house, we unlock the nuanced notes of every bean, bringing the true heart of the farm directly to your table.
